Column Counter

How to play

  • Your ojective is to shift the rows until all of the columns add up to a given total.
  • The total will equal 10 times the amount of rows.
  • You can shift the rows left and right by using the corresponding colored button.
  • Each row overlaps the previous row.
  • This puzzle increases in difficulty as you increase the number of rows and columns.
  • Have Fun!
